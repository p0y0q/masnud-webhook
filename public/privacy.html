Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 01, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MasnudIQ ("we", "our", or "the App") handles data received through the Instagram Graph API and Meta Webhook services. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy and business messaging data are fully protected.

1. Data Processing and No-Storage Policy

Our application operates strictly as a real-time message automation router. When a user sends a message to our integrated Instagram business account, the message is processed dynamically to trigger automated AI customer service responses via secure API endpoints (such as OpenRouter).

  • No Database Storage: We do not store, log, or maintain any personal profiles, chat histories, or user identifiers on our servers.
  • Ephemeral Processing: All event data received via Meta Webhooks is processed instantly in memory and discarded immediately after the execution of the response.

2. Permissions Used

To provide automated conversational support, our application requests access to the following Meta Graph API permissions:

  • instagram_manage_messages: To read and reply to direct messages on behalf of the connected business page.
  • pages_manage_metadata: To manage webhook subscriptions and handle event deliveries.

3. Data Deletion and Opt-Out Instructions

Because we adhere to a strict no-storage policy, there is no persistent user data or user messaging history retained on our infrastructure to delete. However, if you are a business administrator and wish to completely remove our app's access to your Instagram account, you can do so instantly by:

  • Navigating to your Facebook Business/Page Settings.
  • Selecting "Advanced Messaging" or "Business Integrations".
  • Locating MasnudIQ and clicking Remove.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect functional adjustments. Any updates will be published directly on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
